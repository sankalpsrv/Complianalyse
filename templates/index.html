Welcome to the Proof of Concept for Complianalyse

An app which works using LegalRuleML versions of laws to give users an insight into their compliance liabilities.

The advisory on Artificial Intelligence issued by the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology

This was an advisory governing the usage of Artificial Intelligence circulated to a few significant social media intermediaries by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology in the first week of March, 2024.

Since the advisory creates obligations and does so succinctly, it was chosen to demonstrate a proof of concept for a compliance mapping application.

It is hoped that this will enable more people understand the indication that the Government of India is doing in a lucid manner as well as demonstrate the relationships between the concepts denoted.
